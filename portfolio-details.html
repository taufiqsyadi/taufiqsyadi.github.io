Aliyah Luxe | E-Commerce Platform

Aliyah Luxe is a Malaysian "slow fashion" brand based in Kuala Lumpur that is committed to sustainability and the empowerment of modern women through modest, high-quality attire. Its mission focuses on elevating the quality of life for busy career women and business professionals by providing multifunctional, comfortable, and "solat-ready" (prayer-friendly) clothing that requires no ironing.

The brand offers both Ready-to-Wear collections produced in small batches and Made-to-Order luxury services that provide bespoke tailoring with a focus on timeless elegance and ethical craftsmanship. By utilizing eco-friendly materials and prioritizing "quality over quantity," Aliyah Luxe aims to promote mindful consumption while ensuring that women feel confident and sophisticated in versatile designs like their signature Anise suits and Kimono dresses.
