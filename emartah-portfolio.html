eMartah | School Management System

e-MARTAH is a specialized management system designed to streamline the daily operations of the MARTAH educational institution. It integrates several key modules to enhance administrative efficiency and communication.

Core Modules & Functionalities

  • Pickup / Dismissal Manages the daily logistics of student arrivals and departures safely and efficiently.
  • Transport Tracks and organizes student transportation services and routes.
  • E-Ujrah Handles financial matters, likely focusing on fee collections, payments, or staff honorariums.
  • Teacher Notes A dedicated space for educators to record observations, student progress, or lesson remarks.
  • QR Generator / Scan Utilizes QR technology for quick identification, secure check-ins, or data retrieval.
  • Attendance Automates the tracking of student and staff presence to ensure accurate record-keeping.

Summary: In essence, e-MARTAH serves as a centralized digital hub that simplifies school logistics, financial management, and academic documentation, ensuring a smoother workflow for both staff and parents.
